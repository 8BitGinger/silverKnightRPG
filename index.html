Welcome to Silver Knight RPG

a Text Based Node.js Game

The Silver Knights are the last hope for the Foglands.

The Werewolf kind have invaded the Foglands and are terrorizing the villagers. The Silver Knight has sworn an oath to protect the Foglands and to rid the land of the Werewolf kind let by Crux Moonscar.

Choose wisely, as the outcome of the story and the fate of the Foglands are in your hands!

Explore the woods around the Foglands!

Search for Clues to help along the way!

Discover exotic and forgotten places!

and Defeat the Wolf-Lord Crux Moonscar!

Game Description

Silver Knight is a text based RPG game.

You will be playing as a knight on a quest to save the Foglands from the invading Werewolf kind and to fulfill your oath as a Silver Knight.

You will have to make choices that will affect the outcome of the story. Each inquirer prompt will have choices preseneted and a text prompt explaining the scenario. Certain locations hold clues, secrets, or even itesm that will help you on your quest. Just like most adventures it will be treacherous and even Silver Knights rarely survive. Choose wisely, as not just your life, but the fate of the Foglands rests in your hands.

Installation Instructions

First Clone the Repo from this link:
Silver Knight RPG Repo

Then run the following commands in your terminal:

npm install

then run:

npm run start

Follow the instructions in the terminal to play the game.

Easy to install, easy to play!

The Clone Repo also contains the repo for this landing page. You can erase index.html, assets folder, script.js, and the style.css to save storage space!
